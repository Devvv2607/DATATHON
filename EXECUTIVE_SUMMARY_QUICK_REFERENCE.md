# Executive Summary - Quick Reference Card

## What is an Executive Summary?
A business-friendly overview of simulation results that answers: "For this trend, what are the chances of success?"

## The 8 Key Sections

### 1. TREND ANALYSIS
**What it tells you**: Where is the trend in its lifecycle?
- Lifecycle Stage (Emerging ‚Üí Growth ‚Üí Peak ‚Üí Decline ‚Üí Dormant)
- Current Risk Score (0-100)
- Risk Trend (Improving/Stable/Worsening)

**Key Question**: Is this trend in a good position?

### 2. SUCCESS PROBABILITY
**What it tells you**: What's the likelihood of financial success?
- Break-Even Probability (0-100%)
- Success Level (Very High ‚Üí High ‚Üí Moderate ‚Üí Low ‚Üí Very Low)
- Expected ROI Range

**Key Question**: Will we make money?

### 3. FINANCIAL OUTLOOK
**What it tells you**: What are the financial scenarios?
- Best Case ROI
- Worst Case ROI
- Expected ROI
- Outlook (Positive/Moderate/Negative)

**Key Question**: What's the financial upside and downside?

### 4. RISK ASSESSMENT
**What it tells you**: How risky is this scenario?
- Current Risk Score
- Projected Risk Range
- Risk Trend
- Tolerance Alignment

**Key Question**: Does this fit our risk appetite?

### 5. STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATION
**What it tells you**: What should we do?
- Recommended Posture (SCALE/MONITOR/TEST_SMALL/AVOID)
- Overall Outlook (Favorable/Risky/Unfavorable)
- Confidence Level
- Rationale

**Key Question**: What's the recommended action?

### 6. KEY DRIVERS
**What it tells you**: What matters most?
- Growth Ranges (Engagement & Reach)
- Primary Opportunities (Top 3)
- Primary Risks (Top 3)
- Most Sensitive Assumption

**Key Question**: What are the key success factors?

### 7. CRITICAL ASSUMPTIONS
**What it tells you**: What are we assuming?
- Engagement Trend Assumption
- Creator Participation Assumption
- Market Noise Assumption
- Data Coverage & Quality

**Key Question**: How confident are we in our assumptions?

### 8. ACTION ITEMS
**What it tells you**: What should we do next?
- Prioritized Actions (HIGH/MEDIUM)
- Rationale for Each
- Implementation Guidance

**Key Question**: What are the next steps?
