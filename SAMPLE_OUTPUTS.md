## Decision Matrix

### When to Scale vs Pivot vs Exit

| Growth Rate | Classification | Confidence Threshold | Action | Budget Change | Timeline |
|-------------|---------------|---------------------|--------|---------------|----------|
| **> +5%** | Growing | > 70% | Scale & Invest | +20-30% | Immediate |
| **-5% to +5%** | Stable | > 60% | Monitor & Maintain | No change | Monthly review |
| **-5% to -20%** | Declining | > 70% | Try Revival or Pivot | -20% to 0% | 2-3 months |
| **< -20%** | Declining | > 70% | EXIT Immediately | Reallocate 100% | 1 month |

## Key Metrics Explained

### Growth Rate Interpretation

| Growth Rate | Meaning | Urgency |
|-------------|---------|---------|
| **+20% or more** | Explosive growth | Act immediately - high opportunity |
| **+10% to +20%** | Strong growth | Scale quickly - good opportunity |
| **+5% to +10%** | Moderate growth | Scale cautiously - monitor closely |
| **-5% to +5%** | Stable/Flat | Maintain - watch for changes |
| **-10% to -5%** | Moderate decline | Plan pivot - 3-6 month window |
| **-20% to -10%** | Strong decline | Pivot now - 1-3 month window |
| **Below -20%** | Severe decline | Exit immediately - high risk |
